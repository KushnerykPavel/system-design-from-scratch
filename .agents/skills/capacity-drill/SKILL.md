# Capacity Drill

Activation:
- `/capacity-drill <topic>`

Use this as a short review unit when the learner's recurring weakness is `no_sizing` or when `sizing` is the weakest feedback dimension.

Ask the learner to estimate:
- users
- peak QPS
- storage growth
- bandwidth
- rough cost

Then critique:
- missing assumptions
- arithmetic mistakes
- numbers that do not influence the architecture

# Tradeoff Drill

Activation:
- `/tradeoff-drill <topic>`

Use this as a short review unit when the learner's recurring weakness is `weak_tradeoffs` or `weak_consistency_reasoning`, or when `trade_offs` is the weakest feedback dimension.

Prompt format:
- present two or three viable designs
- ask the learner to choose one
- require them to state what they are sacrificing

Push especially on:
- cost vs latency
- consistency vs availability
- operational simplicity vs flexibility

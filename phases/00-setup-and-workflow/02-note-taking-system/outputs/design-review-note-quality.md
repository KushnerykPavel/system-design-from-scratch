Review the note against these questions:

1. Can someone else recover the prompt, scope, and top constraints quickly?
2. Are the sizing assumptions written before architecture choices?
3. Is at least one deliberate trade-off recorded?
4. Are failure modes and observability represented?
5. Does the note capture what the learner missed or would redesign?

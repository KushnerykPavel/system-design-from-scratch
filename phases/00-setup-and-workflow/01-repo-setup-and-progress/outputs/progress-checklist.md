- `.progress.json` exists and is valid
- progress schema version is explicit or intentionally legacy-compatible
- lesson statuses use a controlled vocabulary

- quiz, confidence, and review metadata can be stored without extra tooling
- assisted work is distinguished from unassisted completion
- next lesson selection is deterministic

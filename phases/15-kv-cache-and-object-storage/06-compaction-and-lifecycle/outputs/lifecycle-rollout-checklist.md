# Lifecycle Rollout Checklist

- Dry-run the rule against recent production metadata before activation.
- Confirm retention and legal-hold exceptions are modeled explicitly.
- Estimate transition and deletion backlog generated by the new rule.
- Cap background bandwidth before large transitions begin.
- Add dashboards for rule-specific actions, failures, and skipped objects.
- Require audit logging for destructive actions.
- Stage rollout by tenant, bucket, or prefix instead of flipping globally first.

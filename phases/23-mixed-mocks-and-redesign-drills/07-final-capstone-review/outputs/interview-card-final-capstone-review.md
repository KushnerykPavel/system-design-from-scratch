# Interview Card — Final Capstone Review

## Opening Structure

1. clarify the contract
2. size the dominant path
3. present the architecture
4. choose one deep dive
5. name one alternative you rejected
6. revise after critique
7. close with rollout and observability

## What Reviewers Commonly Challenge

- product fit
- hotspot or scale assumptions
- consistency boundary
- rollout safety
- incident containment
- cost realism

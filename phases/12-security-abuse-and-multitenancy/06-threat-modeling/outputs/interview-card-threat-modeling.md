# Interview Card — Threat Modeling for Interview Design

## Strong answer shape

- Start with assets, actors, and boundaries.
- Pick the top threats, not every threat.
- Change the design because of the threats.
- Add one sentence on observability and one on accepted risk.

## Common misses

- Threat taxonomy with no architecture consequence.
- Only external attackers considered.
- No prioritization.
- No control verification or audit story.

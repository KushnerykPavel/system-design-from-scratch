# Threat Model Worksheet

## Assets

- What data, action, or capacity is most valuable?
- What would hurt most if exposed, altered, or exhausted?

## Actors and paths

- Who might attack or misuse the system?
- Which boundaries do they cross?
- Which paths are both realistic and high impact?

## Mitigations

- Which 2 to 4 controls most improve the design?
- What trade-offs do those controls introduce?
- What metric or audit trail proves they work?

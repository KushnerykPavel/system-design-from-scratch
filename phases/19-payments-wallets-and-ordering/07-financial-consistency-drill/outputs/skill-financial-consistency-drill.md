## Drill opening

- State the core invariant in one sentence.
- Name the source of truth.
- Name the dominant retry or ambiguity risk.

## Must-size numbers

- Steady-state writes
- Retry amplification during incidents
- Retention or reconciliation volume
- Hottest-key concentration if relevant

## Close strong

- One failure table
- One observability plan
- One redesign with an explicit cost or guarantee change

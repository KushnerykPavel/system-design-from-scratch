# Interview Card — Runbooks and First-Response Workflow

## Mention these pieces

- trigger and severity
- first impact check
- first dashboards
- safe mitigations
- explicit escalation criteria
- communication expectations
- recovery validation

## Good phrasing

- "The first ten minutes should not depend on memory."
- "I would separate safe reversible mitigations from expert-only actions."
- "Runbooks should be updated after incidents and architectural changes."

# Interview Card — Alert Design and Paging Quality

## Strong answer shape

- page on symptoms first
- ticket on slower trends
- dedupe likely shared-cause noise
- include ownership and first action
- use short and long burn windows for SLO alerts

## Common anti-patterns

- paging on every infrastructure threshold
- no distinction between urgent and non-urgent alerts
- alerts without runbook or owner
- one dependency outage paging every downstream team independently

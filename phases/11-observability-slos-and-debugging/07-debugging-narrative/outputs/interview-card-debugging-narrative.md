# Interview Card — Incident Debugging Narrative

## Answer pattern

1. confirm user impact
2. narrow scope
3. name plausible cause buckets
4. choose the highest-signal evidence
5. apply the safest mitigation
6. validate recovery
7. record follow-up

## Anti-patterns

- random walk through dashboards
- declaring root cause before comparing hypotheses
- risky irreversible mitigation under uncertainty
- ending with mitigation but no validation plan

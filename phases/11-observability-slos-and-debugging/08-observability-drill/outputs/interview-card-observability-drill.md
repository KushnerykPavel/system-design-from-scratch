# Interview Card — Observability Drill

## Fast answer shape

1. define the primary SLI and SLO
2. choose the metrics that narrow failure causes
3. define trace and log correlation
4. design the first-response dashboard
5. page on user harm, not raw noise
6. explain the runbook and debugging flow
7. summarize cost and cardinality trade-offs

## Interview pushback to prepare for

- "Why is that the right SLI?"
- "Which labels are safe?"
- "What pages at 3 a.m.?"
- "How do you debug one bad region?"
- "What changes at 10x scale?"

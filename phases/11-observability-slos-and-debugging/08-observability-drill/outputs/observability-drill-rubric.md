# Observability Drill Rubric

## Core scoring areas

- primary user-facing SLI
- explicit SLO and budget thinking
- high-signal metrics
- logs, traces, and correlation design
- dashboard structure and label discipline
- page-worthy alert policy
- runbook or first-response workflow
- debugging narrative
- trade-off summary

## Weak answer signs

- observability listed as tools only
- no user-facing objective
- no cost or cardinality discussion
- no incident workflow
- no explanation of what gets paged and why

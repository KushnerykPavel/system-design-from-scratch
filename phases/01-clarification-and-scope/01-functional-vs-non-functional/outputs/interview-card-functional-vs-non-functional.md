## Opening move

- state the primary workflow in one sentence
- separate user-visible capabilities from quality constraints
- rank the top non-functional drivers before drawing architecture

## Fast prompts

- what must the system do?
- what must it be good at?
- which quality constraint changes the design most?
- what can wait until v2?

## Senior signal

- names trade-offs between latency, cost, durability, consistency, and availability
- does not treat every requirement as equally urgent
- ties architecture choices back to named constraints

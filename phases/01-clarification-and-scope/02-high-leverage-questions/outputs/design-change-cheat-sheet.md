- `read-heavy or write-heavy?` changes cache and storage emphasis
- `synchronous or async writes?` changes queue and consistency choices
- `single-region or multi-region?` changes replication and failover design
- `strict isolation or pooled tenants?` changes data model and quota enforcement
- `low latency or low cost?` changes caching, replication, and edge placement
- `strong consistency or eventual consistency?` changes read path and acknowledgement strategy

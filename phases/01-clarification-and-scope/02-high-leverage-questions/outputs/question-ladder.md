## Ask in this order

1. workload shape
2. dominant consistency or freshness need
3. failure scope or regional requirement
4. isolation, abuse, or tenancy concern
5. explicit v1 scope cut if still unclear

## Stop when

- the main read/write pattern is clear
- one dominant non-functional driver is known
- the first architecture sketch has become obvious

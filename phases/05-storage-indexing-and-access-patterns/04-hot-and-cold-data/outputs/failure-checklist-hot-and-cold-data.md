## Hotspot checks
- Can one tenant or key dominate traffic?
- Do we have per-key or per-tenant skew metrics?
- Is the current working set explicitly identified?

## Tiering checks
- What qualifies data as hot, warm, or cold?
- How is data restored from archive?
- Have restore drills been tested?

## Cost checks
- What percentage of data serves most reads?
- Is cold data still paying premium storage costs?

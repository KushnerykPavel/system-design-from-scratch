---
name: four-step-loop-design-review
description: Review checklist for whether a system design answer followed the intended pacing and senior-level structure.
phase: 03
lesson: 01
---

Review the answer against these questions:

1. Did the candidate clarify users, scale, and top non-functional constraints first?
2. Did the candidate size before making architectural claims?
3. Did the candidate get buy-in on a high-level design before deep diving?
4. Was the deep dive chosen deliberately and tied to the main constraint?
5. Did the answer reach failure modes, observability, and trade-offs before time ran out?

## Default split

- clarify: 5-7 min
- size: 4-6 min
- architecture: 10-12 min
- deep dive: 12-15 min
- wrap-up: 5-7 min

## Recovery moves

- summarize assumptions out loud
- cut secondary deep dives
- move to risks earlier than feels comfortable
- preserve at least one minute for trade-off summary

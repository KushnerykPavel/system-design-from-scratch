# Design Review Prompt - Google Strong Signals

Use this after any practice answer.

1. Did the answer clarify scope before naming infrastructure?
2. Did at least one estimate change the design materially?
3. Was the deep dive selected deliberately and justified?
4. Did the answer explain one likely failure and how it would be detected?
5. Did the candidate discuss one important trade-off honestly?
6. Did the redesign answer preserve the original logic while adapting to the new constraint?

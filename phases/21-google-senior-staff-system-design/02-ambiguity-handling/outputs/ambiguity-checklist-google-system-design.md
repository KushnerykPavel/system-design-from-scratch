# Ambiguity Checklist - Google System Design

## Ask First

- who are the primary users?
- what is the main workflow?
- what scale actually matters?
- what guarantee matters most?

## Then State

- one or two core assumptions
- one clear scope cut
- one top non-functional priority

## Avoid

- long discovery interviews
- hidden assumptions
- broad feature lists without prioritization
- architecture before workload shape

# Skill - Google Full Mock

## Mock Flow

1. clarify scope and choose a primary workload
2. rank the top requirements
3. estimate traffic, storage, and growth
4. propose the high-level architecture
5. choose one deep dive and defend it
6. close with risks, observability, rollout, and redesign

## Review Questions

1. Did the answer become concrete fast enough?
2. Did any estimate materially shape the architecture?
3. Was the deep dive the highest-leverage one?
4. Did the closing include both failures and detection?
5. Did redesign change something real?

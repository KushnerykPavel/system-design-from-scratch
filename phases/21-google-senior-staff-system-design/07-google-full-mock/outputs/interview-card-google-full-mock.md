# Interview Card - Google Full Mock

## Minute Markers

- minute 0-5: clarify and scope
- minute 5-10: estimate
- minute 10-20: high-level design
- minute 20-30: deep dive
- minute 30-40: failure and observability
- minute 40-45: redesign and wrap-up

## Must-Have Signals

- one clear workload shape
- one useful estimate
- one deliberate deep dive
- one likely failure plus detection signal
- one honest redesign trade-off

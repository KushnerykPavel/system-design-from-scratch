# Capacity Drill

Activation:
- `/capacity-drill <topic>`

Ask the learner to estimate:
- users
- peak QPS
- storage growth
- bandwidth
- rough cost

Then critique:
- missing assumptions
- arithmetic mistakes
- numbers that do not influence the architecture

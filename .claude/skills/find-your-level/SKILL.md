# Find Your Level

Give the learner a 10-question placement quiz across:
- clarification and scope
- estimation
- architecture building blocks
- consistency and reliability
- system-specific design

## Output

- recommended starting phase
- why that phase is the right entry point
- 3 lesson recommendations
- one recurring weakness to watch
